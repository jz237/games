How To Play

This version keeps the original Qix loop: safe movement on existing walls, dangerous movement while drawing, and area capture determined by which side traps the Qix.

Controls: drag anywhere on the field. Your current direction stays active while you hold. On desktop, use arrow keys or WASD.
Safe movement: move along glowing walls and captured territory edges with no risk from the Qix.
Danger movement: when you leave safety, your tracer becomes vulnerable. If the Qix line touches it, you lose a life.
Scoring: bigger captures score more, and each level raises Qix speed and the target percentage.
Local + Global Scores

Local scores stay on your device. Global scores sync to the shared leaderboard.
