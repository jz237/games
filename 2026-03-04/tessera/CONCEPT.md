# TESSERA

## Title
Tessera

## Premise
A hypnotic hexagonal territory war. You and an AI battle to claim glowing hex tiles on a honeycomb board. Each tile you claim pulses outward, triggering chain reactions when you surround enemy tiles. The AI grows more aggressive with each level — watch your flanks.

## Visual Style
- Deep space background with subtle nebula gradient
- Hexagonal grid with neon glow borders
- Player tiles: electric blue with inner light pulse
- AI tiles: crimson with flicker effect
- Particle bursts on tile captures
- Screen shake on chain captures
- Smooth animations with easing

## Mechanic
- Tap empty hex tiles to claim them (costs 1 action point, regenerates over time)
- Surround any 3 sides of an enemy tile to flip it (capture)
- Chain captures trigger bonus particles
- Each level: larger board + faster AI + AI uses smarter flanking strategy
- Win by controlling 60% of the board
