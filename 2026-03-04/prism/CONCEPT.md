# PRISM 🔮

## Title
Prism — Laser Crystal Redirect Puzzle

## Premise
You're a light physicist in a crystal cave. Lasers shoot from emitters on the walls — your job is to rotate the scattered prisms and mirrors to guide the beams onto their targets. Chain reflections, split beams through prisms, light everything up before time runs out.

## Visual Style
- Deep indigo/black background with faint hex grid
- Laser beams: vivid neon (red/green/blue/yellow) with soft glow halos and particle sparks at every bounce
- Mirrors: angular metallic gems with glint animation on rotation
- Prisms: triangular crystalline forms that visibly split light
- Targets: glowing hollow circles that pulse and fill with color when hit
- Level complete: cascading light burst with crystal shatter particles

## Core Mechanics
- Grid-based board (8×8)
- Emitters shoot lasers in fixed directions (cannot rotate)
- Mirrors: tap to rotate 45° — reflect the beam
- Prisms: tap to rotate 45° — split beam into two perpendicular outputs
- Targets must ALL be lit to complete the level
- Beams are traced in real-time after every rotation
- 3 levels: increasing crystal count, multi-target chains, crossed beams

## What Makes It Weird
A single prism can be hit by two different colored lasers, merging them into a mixed-color hybrid beam that only activates certain "resonance targets."
