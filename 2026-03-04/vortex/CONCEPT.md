# VORTEX 🌀

## Title
Vortex — Storm Eater

## Premise
You ARE the storm. A living vortex tears across a surreal sky filled with swirling debris — glowing energy orbs in four elemental colors. Drag your vortex to devour matching orbs, grow stronger, and avoid being poisoned by the wrong ones. The storm grows wilder with each level.

## Visual Style
- Deep purple/black sky with aurora-like background shimmer
- Player vortex: spinning spiral arms with inner glow, color-tinted by active mode
- Orbs: translucent glowing spheres with color-coded particle trails (red=fire, blue=ice, green=lightning, gold=void)
- Absorption: orb spirals into vortex with satisfying swirl animation
- Wrong absorption: vortex flashes red, screen trembles
- Level up: vortex expands dramatically, shockwave pulse rings out

## Core Mechanics
- Canvas-based free movement (drag to move vortex)
- Orbs spawn at screen edges, drift inward with gentle curve
- Vortex has a current "color mode" — shown as tint in the spinning arms
- Absorb matching color orb → +10 points, vortex grows slightly
- Absorb wrong color → -1 health (5 max), vortex shrinks slightly
- Tap vortex to manually cycle color mode (it also auto-cycles every 8 seconds)
- Level 2: faster orbs, more types; Level 3: orbs chain-react, combos matter

## What Makes It Weird
When the vortex gets big enough, it starts warping the screen around it — orbs visually bend toward it even before contact.
