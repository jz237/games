# RUNECASTER

## Title
Runecaster

## Premise
A mystical duel between two wizards. Draw rune symbols on the screen with your finger — the shape you draw determines which spell fires. Lightning bolt, fireball, shield, vortex. The AI wizard draws its own runes (visible, so you can counter). Match your spell to counter the AI's, or be the first to deal 3 hits. Timing matters — draw faster to interrupt.

## Visual Style
- Dark parchment/arcane background with floating star particles
- Glowing trail follows finger as you draw
- Rune recognition feedback: gold flash for correct, red for mis-drawn
- Spell effects: lightning crackle, fireball bloom, shield shimmer ring, vortex spiral
- HP orbs (glowing spheres) for health tracking
- Hit effects: screen flash + shake + particle explosion
- AI wizard silhouette on opposite side

## Mechanic
- Swipe the rune glyph pattern shown in the hint panel
- 4 runes: Triangle (fireball), Zigzag (lightning), Circle (shield), S-curve (vortex)
- Spells interact: Lightning beats Shield, Fireball beats Vortex, Vortex beats Lightning, Shield beats Fireball
- Each level: smaller hint time, faster AI drawing, more complex rune combinations
- Win 3 rounds to beat the level
