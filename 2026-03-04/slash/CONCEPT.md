# SLASH ⚔️

## Title
Slash — Rune Cutter

## Premise
Ancient rune-creatures are converging on the sacred seal at the center of the screen. Your weapon is a single swipe. Draw a line with your finger — anything that intersects with your blade gets destroyed. Big slashes, combo chains, rune-carved explosions. The circles you draw summon divine bombs. Everything is made of light and dark energy.

## Visual Style
- Black velvet background with subtle swirling ink patterns
- Enemies: glowing polygonal rune-creatures in jewel tones (amethyst, jade, topaz, ruby)
- Slash trail: luminous white-gold ink that hangs in the air 0.4s then fades
- Kill effect: shatter into rune-fragment particles that scatter and fade
- Circle bomb: ring of fire expands from center, incinerating everything it touches
- Sacred seal (center): ancient mandala slowly rotating, flashes red when hit
- Screen edge spawn: enemies drift inward in slow spirals

## Core Mechanics
- Enemies spawn continuously at edges, spiral toward center
- Player swipes to draw cut lines (any angle, any length)
- Line is checked against all enemy hitboxes — everything it crosses dies
- Longer cuts that hit more enemies = higher combo multiplier
- Draw a rough circle (start ≈ end point) = area bomb (wipes screen), 15s cooldown
- Center takes damage if an enemy reaches it (5 HP)
- 3 levels: more enemies, faster movement, special shielded enemies needing 2 hits

## What Makes It Weird
Some enemies leave "rune echoes" when killed — ghost images that drift on and can still deal damage if you're not watching. Cut those too.
