HOW TO PLAY
Aim and fire: hold anywhere on the playfield to aim the cannon. The train auto-fires while you hold.
Switch tracks: touch and drag left or right, then release. A clear horizontal swipe jumps your train one lane in that direction.
Shield Burst: touch and swipe upward, then release to trigger a short defensive surge. It gives you a brief safety window, helps you slip through danger, and recharges on a cooldown.
Collect shards: grab the bright glowing orb shards dropped by destroyed enemies for score and upgrade momentum.
Read the fleet: Interceptors vector-burn, Bulwarks shield, Gunships fire triple volleys, Carriers launch fighters, Wraiths phase, and Bombers seed void mines. The Leviathan combines siege weapons with its own fighter screen.
Desktop: aim with the mouse anywhere on screen and hold the button to fire. A/D or ←/→ switch tracks, Space (or W/↑) fires Shield Burst, 1–3 pick upgrades, Enter/Esc drive the menus, M toggles mute.
