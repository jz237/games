# Magma Surfer — Design Doc

## Concept Selection

### Concept A: Magma Surfer ⭐ SELECTED
- **Core Mechanic:** Endless downhill lava surfing. Player rides a chunk of obsidian down a river of flowing lava. Swipe left/right to dodge volcanic rocks, lava geysers, and narrow canyon walls. Tap to jump over obstacles. Hold to crouch/speed boost through low overhangs.
- **Visual Style:** Deep volcanic palette — molten orange/yellow lava, black obsidian, red ember particles, heat shimmer distortion effect. The lava surface undulates with sine-wave animation. Parallax volcanic canyon walls. Massive particle systems for lava splashes, ember trails, geyser eruptions.
- **Controls:** Swipe/drag left-right to steer. Tap to jump. Hold to crouch/boost. All touch-primary.
- **Difficulty Curve:** Starts with wide lava river, few obstacles. Speed increases gradually. Obstacles get denser. Geysers start erupting. Canyon narrows. Lava waves become choppier.
- **Visual Wow:** 3D perspective view down the lava river. Heat distortion shader. Massive particle eruptions. Glowing crystal pickups. The entire screen pulses with volcanic energy.

### Concept B: Constellation Weaver
- Draw lines between stars to form constellations before they fade. Night sky with nebula, aurora effects. Tap-and-drag. Too similar to puzzle games already in catalog.

### Concept C: Hex Fortress
- You are a turret on a hex grid, enemies approach from all 6 directions. Tap sectors to fire. Neon geometric style. Fun but might feel static.

## Why Magma Surfer Wins
- Most visceral and visually impressive
- Intuitive touch controls everyone understands
- Endless runner format = addictive score chasing
- Volcanic theme is underrepresented in the catalog
- Heavy particle opportunities = visual stunner
