# Photon Dash — Design Doc

## Concept Candidates

### 1. Photon Dash (SELECTED)
- **Core Mechanic:** You ARE a beam of light racing through a crystal corridor. The beam auto-advances. You swipe to refract at crystal prism junctions — choosing left/right/split paths. Collect spectrum fragments (R,O,Y,G,B,I,V) to fill your rainbow meter. Miss a junction = you scatter (die). The corridor speeds up and branches get tighter.
- **Visual Style:** Black void background. Your beam is a thick glowing white line that splits into rainbow prismatic trails. Crystal prisms are translucent 3D-looking hexagonal shapes with internal refraction effects. Collected spectrum pieces create trailing aurora effects. Heavy bloom/glow. The entire screen pulses with color.
- **Controls:** Swipe left/right at junctions to choose path. Tap to activate "focus mode" (slow-mo for 2 seconds, limited uses). Double-tap to split beam at Y-junctions (advanced mechanic).
- **Difficulty:** Starts slow with obvious junctions, 2-way splits. Adds 3-way splits, fake paths (that lead to walls), speed increases, moving prisms, and dark zones where you can't see ahead.
- **Phone wow-factor:** The rainbow light trails filling the screen, prismatic particle explosions, the rush of speed with bloom effects everywhere.

### 2. Hex Fortress
- Tower defense on hex grid. Place fractal towers, geometric enemies flow in. Upgrade towers to grow more complex fractal patterns. Visually cool but TD is slow-paced and less immediately thrilling on mobile.

### 3. Warp Weaver
- Draw portals to redirect falling orbs into color-matched collectors. Physics puzzle-action. Fun but portal-drawing on small screens is finicky.

## Decision: Photon Dash
The light-beam refraction concept is the most visually unique, has simple swipe controls perfect for mobile, and the speed-ramping creates natural tension. Nothing in the existing catalog does light/refraction mechanics.
