# Shatter Storm — Full Build Specification

## Overview
Single-file HTML5 canvas game. Breakout/brick-breaker with stained-glass aesthetic. Mobile-first touch controls. 60fps.

---

## SCREENS & STATE MACHINE

### States: TITLE → PLAYING → PAUSED → GAME_OVER → HIGH_SCORE_ENTRY → TITLE

### Title Screen
- Animated background: slow-rotating kaleidoscope of glass shards
- Title "SHATTER STORM" in gothic font (canvas-drawn, glowing white with colored shadow)
- "TAP TO START" pulsing text
- High scores table (top 10) displayed below
- Tap anywhere → start game at level 1

### Playing Screen
- Top bar: Score (left), Level (center), Lives (right, shown as paddle icons)
- Game area: full remaining screen
- Paddle at bottom, ball in play, bricks in patterns
- When ball stuck on paddle: "TAP TO LAUNCH" text above paddle

### Pause
- Tap top-right pause icon → overlay with "PAUSED" + "RESUME" button + "QUIT" button
- Resume → back to PLAYING, Quit → TITLE

### Game Over
- "GAME OVER" text with glass-shatter animation
- Final score + level display
- If top-10 score → transition to HIGH_SCORE_ENTRY
- Else → "TAP TO CONTINUE" → TITLE

### High Score Entry
- 3-letter initials with ▲/▼ touch buttons per letter
- "SAVE" button
- localStorage key: "shatter_storm_high_scores"

## GAME OBJECTS

### Paddle
- Width: 20% of canvas width (grows/shrinks with power-ups)
- Height: 12px, rounded corners
- Gradient fill: bright cyan to white center glow
- Subtle glow/bloom effect (shadow blur)
- Movement: follows touch X position on bottom 60% of screen
- Clamped to screen edges

### Ball
- Radius: 8px
- Gradient fill: white center, cyan edge
- Trail: last 12 positions drawn as fading circles
- Speed: starts at 5px/frame, increases 0.3 per level (cap at 10)
- Launch angle: random between 60°-120° upward
- Bounces off walls, ceiling, paddle, bricks
- Paddle bounce angle based on hit position (left edge = sharp left, center = straight up, right = sharp right)
- If ball falls below paddle → lose life, ball resets on paddle

### Bricks
- Size: calculated to fill pattern area (roughly 8-12 columns, 6-10 rows)
- Each brick: rounded rect with translucent fill + white border (glass look)
- Colors from palette: ruby red, sapphire blue, emerald green, amber gold, amethyst purple, topaz orange
- Hit points: 1 (normal), 2 (darker shade, crack overlay after 1st hit), 3 (steel-bordered)
- Steel bricks: gray, indestructible, metallic gradient
- On hit: flash white for 2 frames
- On break: spawn 5-8 glass shard particles + score popup (+10/+20/+30 based on HP)

### Glass Shard Particles
- Triangular shapes, random size 3-8px
- Color matches broken brick with slight variation
- Initial velocity: burst outward from brick center, random angles
- Gravity: 0.15 downward
- Rotation: random spin rate
- Alpha: fade from 0.8 to 0 over 60 frames
- Some shards have white specular highlight (sparkle)

### Power-Ups
- 15% chance to drop from broken brick
- Fall downward at 2px/frame
- Catch with paddle to activate
- Types:
  - WIDE (green, "W"): paddle 1.5x width for 10 seconds
  - MULTI (blue, "M"): spawn 2 extra balls
  - FIRE (red, "F"): ball passes through bricks for 5 seconds (no bounce, just destroys)
  - SLOW (yellow, "S"): ball 0.5x speed for 8 seconds
- Visual: glowing orb with letter, pulsing, trailing sparkles as it falls
- Timer bar shown at top when active

### Combo System
- Track rapid hits (within 30 frames of each other)
- Combo counter displayed center screen, large, fading
- Multiplier: x1 (1 hit), x2 (3 hits), x3 (5 hits), x4 (8 hits), x5 (12+ hits)
- Score = base_score × multiplier
- Screen shake intensity scales with combo (1px at x2, 3px at x5)

## LEVEL GENERATION

### Patterns (procedural with templates)
- Level 1: Simple 6×4 grid, all 1HP, pastel colors
- Level 2: Diamond shape, mixed 1-2HP
- Level 3: Circle/ring pattern
- Level 4: Cross/plus pattern with steel border
- Level 5 (BOSS): Bricks slowly move left-right
- Levels 6+: Combine patterns, more steel, more HP, moving rows
- Pattern generated by mathematical functions (sin, cos for curves; modulo for checkerboards)
- Each level has a dominant color theme

### Level Transition
- When all breakable bricks destroyed → "LEVEL COMPLETE" text
- Remaining shards sweep upward
- 1-second pause, then new pattern fades in
- Ball resets to paddle

## VISUAL EFFECTS

### Background
- Near-black (#0a0a14) base
- 2-3 volumetric light rays: diagonal lines from top, very subtle (alpha 0.03), slowly rotating
- Faint grid of dots (alpha 0.05) for depth
- Background hue shifts slightly per level

### Screen Shake
- On combo x2+: random offset applied to entire canvas transform
- Duration: 6 frames, intensity decays
- On life lost: bigger shake (5px, 10 frames)

### Ball Trail
- Store last 12 ball positions
- Draw each as circle with decreasing alpha and size
- Creates comet effect

### Glow Effects
- Ball: shadowBlur 15, shadowColor cyan
- Paddle: shadowBlur 10, shadowColor cyan
- Power-ups: shadowBlur 20, color matches type
- Score popups: shadowBlur 8, gold

### Score Popups
- Float upward from broken brick position
- "+10" / "+20×3" etc.
- Gold color, fade out over 40 frames
- Scale from 1.0 to 0.5 as they rise

## AUDIO (Web Audio API)

All sounds generated with oscillators, no external files.

- **Paddle hit**: short blip, 440Hz sine, 50ms decay
- **Brick hit**: higher pitch based on brick color (500-800Hz), short, with noise burst for glass effect
- **Brick break**: noise burst (white noise filtered) + descending tone, 100ms — sounds like glass breaking
- **Power-up collect**: ascending arpeggio (3 quick tones rising)
- **Life lost**: descending tone, 300ms, sad
- **Level complete**: major chord arpeggio, 500ms
- **Game over**: minor chord, slow, 800ms
- **Combo milestone**: quick chime, pitch increases with combo level

## CONTROLS

### Touch (Primary)
- touchstart/touchmove on bottom 60% of screen: move paddle to touch X
- touchstart on ball-on-paddle state: launch ball
- All touch handlers: check e.target.closest('button') || e.target.closest('.overlay') before preventDefault()
- Buttons get BOTH click AND touchend with stopPropagation

### Mouse (Fallback)
- mousemove: move paddle
- click: launch ball

### Keyboard (Debug)
- Left/Right arrows: move paddle
- Space: launch ball
- P: pause

## HIGH SCORE SYSTEM

- localStorage key: "shatter_storm_scores"
- Array of {initials: "AAA", score: 0, level: 1}
- Top 10, sorted descending by score
- Entry screen: 3 columns of ▲/letter/▼, each letter A-Z cycling
- Large touch targets (48px minimum)
- "SAVE" button below
- New entry highlighted in gold on leaderboard
- Shown on title screen and after game over (if qualifies)

## PERFORMANCE

- Object pooling for particles (pre-allocate 200, recycle)
- Only draw particles with alpha > 0.01
- Brick collision: spatial check only against ball's row/column neighborhood
- requestAnimationFrame with delta time normalization
- Target: 60fps on mid-range mobile
