# Shatter Storm — Design Document

## Concept
A visually stunning breakout/brick-breaker reimagined with stained-glass aesthetics. Players smash through intricate stained-glass window patterns using a glowing energy ball. Each brick shatters into realistic glass shards with refraction effects, creating cascading particle explosions. The paddle is controlled by touch-drag at the bottom.

## Core Mechanic
- Touch-drag paddle left/right to bounce a ball
- Ball breaks stained-glass bricks arranged in elaborate mandala/rose-window patterns
- Each brick shatters into 5-8 translucent glass shards that tumble with physics
- Power-ups drop from special bricks (multiball, wider paddle, fireball, slow-mo)
- Combo system: rapid successive hits multiply score

## Visual Style
- Dark cathedral background with subtle volumetric light rays
- Bricks are colored translucent glass pieces forming beautiful patterns
- Ball leaves a glowing comet trail
- Shatter particles have refraction/sparkle effects
- Screen shake on big combos
- Background shifts color per level

## Control Scheme
- Touch drag anywhere on bottom half to move paddle
- Tap to launch ball from paddle
- No buttons during gameplay — pure touch

## Difficulty Curve
- Level 1: Simple symmetric pattern, slow ball
- Each level: faster ball, more complex patterns, some bricks need 2-3 hits
- Steel bricks (unbreakable) create obstacles
- Boss levels every 5 levels with moving brick formations

## What Makes It Visually Impressive
- Hundreds of glass shard particles with transparency and rotation
- Volumetric light beams from behind the glass
- Each level is a unique stained-glass artwork
- The destruction is deeply satisfying — glass everywhere
- Glow effects on ball, paddle, and power-ups
