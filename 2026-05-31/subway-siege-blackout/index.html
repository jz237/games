Iron Ridge:
Turret Commander

Three.js v1.7

Command a tank from the center of an open battlefield. Drag to traverse the turret 360 degrees and raise or lower the gun. Fire arcing shells across the hills, then move the hull in the direction your turret is pointing.

Controls

Phone: drag the battlefield to aim turret and elevation. Hold Move to drive toward the turret. Fire launches a shell.

Desktop: mouse drag aims, Space fires, W or arrows move, R reloads.
