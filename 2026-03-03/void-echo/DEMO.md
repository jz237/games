# Void Echo — AI vs AI Demo Playthrough

**Setup:** Two AIs share the same arena. AI-Ghost (evasion strategy) vs AI-Striker (aggressive hunt strategy). 60-second survival match.

---

**T+0s — The Dark**
Screen is black. Two tiny white diamonds appear near opposite walls. Three asteroids visible only because the spawn animation briefly illuminated them. Then: nothing. Pure black silence.

---

**T+1.2s — Ghost's First Whisper**
Ghost emits a tiny pulse — minimum power. A thin blue ring expands 120px and dies. It catches one asteroid and the edge of a Void creature. Ghost notes the positions and begins a slow drift away from the Void. No reaction from Striker yet.

---

**T+2.8s — Striker Opens Up**
Striker fires a massive pulse — full power. A brilliant ring blasts out 300px in every direction. The whole arena lights up for half a second: 3 asteroids, 2 Voids, and Ghost's position all revealed in cold blue light. Ghost registers "threat!" and pulses harder to see an escape route.

---

**T+3.1s — Voids Converge**
Both Voids heard Striker's massive pulse. They pivot toward Striker's last position and begin accelerating. Striker sees this on the pulse reveal and starts evasive drift.

---

**T+7s — Ghost Invisible**
Ghost has not pulsed again. Drifting silently in the dark. A Void passes within 60px of Ghost — but without a pulse, neither the Void nor the player knows. Audio plays: a low bass flutter (proximity alert). Ghost is flying blind and alive.

---

**T+9s — Striker Sets a Trap**
Striker fires two quick pulses from slightly different positions — left then right. The intersection zone lights up with constructive interference glow. Void #1 is caught in the overlap. DESTROYED. White flash, crimson particle burst. Score: Ghost 0, Striker 1.

---

**T+14s — The Accident**
Ghost's drift carries it toward an asteroid. Blind in the dark. Ghost pulses to check — the asteroid is 30px away and closing. Ghost banks hard. Just barely clears it. The pulse from the near-miss alerts Void #2, which angles toward Ghost.

---

**T+21s — Cat and Mouse**
Ghost and Void #2 are in a slow dance — Ghost drifting silently, Void following where the last pulse originated. Ghost changes direction. Void overshoots. Ghost has now survived 21 seconds without a kill.

---

**T+29s — Striker Takes Damage**
A new Void spawned at T+20. Striker's aggressive pulsing attracted both new Void and Void #2. Two Voids closing from different angles. Striker tries the double-pulse intersection trick — fires left pulse — but Void #2 isn't in frame. Right pulse misses. Striker takes a hit. Screen flashes red. One life lost (of 3).

---

**T+35s — Ghost Goes Aggressive**
Ghost has been invisible long enough. Energy (in-game abstraction) is high. Ghost fires a large pulse, spots Void #2 directly ahead. Fires second pulse at 45° angle. Intersection catches Void #2. DESTROYED. Score: Ghost 1, Striker 1. The arena went briefly beautiful — two overlapping sonar rings, a crimson explosion, then black.

---

**T+44s — Chaos Phase**
Two new Voids spawned (total 3 active). Striker is pulsing frantically, trying to keep vision. Ghost hears the pulse madness and stays silent. The arena is strobing with Striker's rings. Three Voids locked on Striker.

---

**T+51s — Striker Falls**
Striker attempts a triple intersection (3 active pulses). Destroys one Void. Other two close in from flanks. Striker runs out of room. Death. Score: Ghost 1, Striker 1. Striker has 2 lives left, respawns at edge.

---

**T+57s — Final Seconds**
Ghost drifts toward screen center, fires one careful pulse. Sees one Void dead ahead, one to the right. No clean intersection angle. Ghost dodges both, pulsing minimally. Time running out.

---

**T+60s — Match End**
Soft sustained tone signals round end.

**Final Score:**
- Ghost: 1 Void destroyed, 60s survived, 0 deaths → 160 points
- Striker: 2 Voids destroyed, 51s survived, 1 death → 141 points

**Ghost wins by survival.** 

Post-match replay shows both trajectories as faint white lines on the black field. Ghost's path: elegant arcs and long still drifts. Striker's path: frantic zigzags and bright sonar rings. Opposite philosophies. Both beautiful.
