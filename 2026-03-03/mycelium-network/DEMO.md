# Mycelium Network — AI vs AI Demo Playthrough

**Setup:** 200px petri dish. 8 nutrient nodes scattered randomly. AI-Cyan (left colony) vs AI-Amber (right colony). Round lasts 90 seconds.

---

**T+0s — Germination**
Both colonies appear as single glowing nodes at opposite edges of the dish. Cyan pulses cold blue-green. Amber pulses warm orange. Three nutrient nodes are equidistant, two lean Cyan, three lean Amber.

---

**T+4s — First Growth**
Cyan extends its first tendril toward the nearest nutrient (top-center). The thread grows in real time — a slow, deliberate reach. Amber splits immediately: one tendril north, one south. Aggressive expansion.

---

**T+9s — First Contact with Nutrients**
Cyan's tendril touches Node #3. Amber glow. Nutrients begin flowing. Cyan's whole network brightens slightly — energy arriving. Score: Cyan 1 node, Amber 0.

---

**T+14s — Amber Gets Greedy**
Amber's south tendril connects to Node #6. Then Node #7. Two simultaneous feeds. But Amber's north tendril is overextended — it hasn't reached a node yet and is bleeding energy to maintain itself. Amber's glow dims.

---

**T+22s — The Fatigue**
Amber's north branch reaches 18% energy. It starts to grey out at the tip. Amber AI notices, stops growth there. Too late — the tip curls and dies. The branch retracts in a slow, beautiful death-curl animation.

---

**T+30s — Cyan's Strategic Move**
Cyan now has 3 nodes connected. Energy is strong. Cyan AI detects Node #5 is uncontested but equidistant. It launches a Spore Burst — a single glowing seed shoots across the dish in a parabolic arc. It lands adjacent to Node #5. A new mini-colony sprouts.

---

**T+38s — Collision**
Cyan's eastern tendril and Amber's western tendril approach Node #4 simultaneously. They touch. Energy levels compared: Cyan 67, Amber 41. Amber's tip shrivels and retreats. Cyan absorbs Node #4.

---

**T+47s — Amber Rallies**
Amber consolidates — trims two dying branches, focuses all energy into two fat, fast tendrils racing for the two remaining unclaimed nodes. The efficiency boost is visible: Amber glows brighter, threads thicker.

---

**T+60s — Race for Node #8**
Both AIs target the last unclaimed node simultaneously. Amber is closer by 12px. Amber wins the race. Amber connects. Score: Cyan 4, Amber 3. One node left — Node #2, buried in the center. The dish is now a tangle of glowing threads.

---

**T+74s — The Center Node**
Both colonies have tendrils approaching Node #2 from different angles. It's a two-lane collision course. Cyan arrives first — barely. Node #2 absorbed. Amber collides with Cyan's thread. Massive energy comparison: Cyan 91, Amber 55. Amber's whole eastern tendril DIES. A cascade of grey spreading from the collision point inward.

---

**T+82s — Amber's Collapse**
The cascade kills 3 branches. Amber is now feeding from only 2 nodes. Energy bleeds. Tendrils start curling back everywhere. The warm orange glow fades to brown.

---

**T+90s — Round End**
Siren sounds (soft, organic tone). Network freeze.

**Final Score:**
- Cyan: 5 nodes, 89 energy, 14 living tendrils
- Amber: 3 nodes, 22 energy, 6 living tendrils

**Cyan wins.** The dish is a glowing teal web with collapsed orange ruins threaded through it. Looks incredible.

---

**Post-Game**
The petri dish is preserved as a still image. Playable again from any living node.
