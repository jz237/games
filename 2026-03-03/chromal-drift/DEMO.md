# Chromal Drift — AI vs AI Demo Playthrough

**Level:** "The Mixer" — 3 sources (Red, Yellow, Blue), 5 drains (Orange, Green, Purple, Red, Blue)
**Players:** AI-Alice (optimizer) vs AI-Bob (chaos agent)

---

**Turn 1 — Setup Phase**
Alice surveys the board. Three paint sources at top: Red (left), Yellow (center), Blue (right). Five drain targets at bottom. She calculates: Yellow+Red must merge for Orange, Yellow+Blue for Green, Red+Blue for Purple.

Bob immediately places a Splitter under the Yellow source. "Chaos first," his algorithm whispers.

---

**Turn 2 — Alice Responds**
Alice places a curved wall to intercept Yellow-left, routing it toward Red. A future Orange merge point. She places a second wall routing Yellow-right toward Blue. Canvas is pristine white.

---

**Turn 3 — Flow Begins**
Round starts. Paint flows. Red pours left, Yellow fans both directions from the splitter, Blue falls straight. First particles hit the canvas. A blush of crimson bleeds across the top-left.

---

**Turn 4 — First Mix**
Yellow-left meets Red mid-canvas. A warm bloom erupts — orange. The orange stream finds its drain. Score: Alice 47, Bob 0. The canvas now has a gorgeous sunrise smear across the center-left.

---

**Turn 5 — Bob's Chaos Pays Off**
Bob's splitter also sent Yellow into unexpected territory. A rogue yellow stream curves around Alice's wall and crashes into the Blue stream early. Teal explosion. Not quite Green — the drain spits it back. Overflow begins. Mud meter: 12%.

---

**Turn 6 — Alice Pivots**
Alice activates a redirector she pre-placed (bonus mechanic: one hidden redirector per round). She curves the teal stream back toward the green drain. Close enough match. Score: Alice 89, Bob 31. Canvas is becoming a wild abstract painting.

---

**Turn 7 — Purple Chase**
Red and Blue must merge for Purple. Alice's path is clear. Bob throws a wall in the way — paint splashes. Both streams scatter. The canvas gets a brutal violet splatter across the right side. Mud meter: 28%.

---

**Turn 8 — Bob Scores Surprisingly**
Bob's chaos created an accidental perfect Blue stream that hits the Blue drain untouched. Score: Alice 89, Bob 81. The crowd (imaginary) gasps.

---

**Turn 9 — Final Push**
Alice reassembles the Red+Blue merge using two remaining walls. A clean purple ribbon flows to the drain. Score: Alice 147, Bob 81. Three drains still open. Mud meter rising: 55%.

---

**Turn 10 — Time's Up**
Flow cuts off. Final canvas: a swirling mess of crimson, cobalt, and amber with a gorgeous unintentional mandala in the center where all three streams briefly converged.

**Alice wins: 147–81.** But Bob's painting looks cooler.

---

**The Canvas**
Both players' actions are burned into a single watercolor masterpiece. The game offers: "Export as 4K PNG?" Alice's precision shows in clean color bands. Bob's chaos left wild blooms and halos. Together: unexpectedly beautiful.
